The best movies according to chike

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Stranger things

I enjoyed the fact that this movie is an all teen based movie
with great acting skillsets.

When they see us

Here's another great teen movie that tells us about
a true life story of how a couple of teens were falsely accused
and framed for a murder they knew nothing about, just because
they were black kids living in a white neighbourhood.
Great movie to bring about awareness of false accusation/framing
in the corrupt system of justice.

Lucifer

Love the conception of how the directors of this movie portray
the human existence in relation to the divine.
Great characters too.
